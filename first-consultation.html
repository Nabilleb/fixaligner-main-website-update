Your First Fixaligner Consultation

What to expect during your first visit to a Fixaligner clear aligner consultation

Questions

Considering clear aligners to improve your smile? Congratulations! Taking the first step towards a confident, straighter smile is an exciting decision. To help you feel prepared and at ease, let's walk through what you can expect during your first Fixaligner consultation.

·       Friendly Welcome and Discussion:

Upon arriving at your consultation, you'll be greeted by our friendly Fixaligner team. We'll start by having an open discussion about your smile goals and any concerns you may have. We want to understand your unique needs and desires to ensure Fixaligners are the right fit for you.

·       Comprehensive Examination:

Next, our dental professional will conduct a thorough examination of your teeth and oral health. This may involve taking X-rays, photographs, and digital scans of your teeth. These diagnostic records provide a clear picture of your current dental alignment and help us create a customized treatment plan.

·       Treatment Plan Presentation:

Using advanced 3D technology, we'll show you a digital simulation of your potential smile transformation. You'll be able to see how your teeth will move throughout the treatment process and get a glimpse of your future smile. Our dental professional will explain the recommended treatment duration and answer any questions you may have.

·       Financial Confirmation:

We believe in transparency, so we'll discuss the cost of your Fixaligner treatment and any available financing options. Our team will work with you to find a payment plan that fits your budget.

·       Next Steps:

If you're ready to proceed, we'll guide you through the next steps, which may include taking impressions or scheduling your aligner fitting appointment.

     Your first Fixaligner consultation is an opportunity to learn, ask questions, and envision your dream smile. We're here to support you every step of the way on your journey to a confident, straighter smile with Fixaligners.
