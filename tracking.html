Track Your Progress

Our Tracking app is like your best buddy along the journey to a perfect smile. It helps you stay on top of your treatment all the time.

About this stage

At Fixaligner, we believe in empowering our patients throughout their orthodontic treatment.

Upon receiving your aligner package, we provide you with a user-friendly application to track your progress.

This intuitive app serves as a helpful companion, reminding you when it's time to switch to the next set of aligners.

With personalized notifications and easy-to-follow instructions, you can stay on track with your treatment plan effortlessly.

The app also allows you to visualize your progress, providing a visual representation, of how your smile is transforming over time. We strive to make your orthodontic journey seamless and convenient, giving you the tools, you need to achieve your desired results with confidence.
