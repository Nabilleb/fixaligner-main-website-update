Your Treatment in One Box

Your treatment will comprise a series of aligners sequenced in steps. Every step you need to wear your aligner for 20 hours for 10 days

About this stage

Once the production of your clear aligners is finalized at our site, we take utmost care to provide you with a comprehensive package. You will receive a meticulously organized box containing your complete set of aligners, each one labeled with its corresponding number.

This thoughtful approach ensures that you have a clear understanding of the prescribed order in which to wear the aligners for optimal results.

With all the aligners conveniently packaged and clearly marked, you can effortlessly progress through your treatment plan, knowing exactly when to switch to the next aligner. Our goal is to simplify your orthodontic journey, making it as convenient and user-friendly as possible.
